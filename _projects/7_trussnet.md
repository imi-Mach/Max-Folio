*May 2019 – August 2019 · Baker Institute Eureka! Ventures Pitch Competition Winner*

As a co-founder of **TrussNet**, I helped create an application that guides college students in course selection based on their unique interests. Our tool **won the Baker Institute's Eureka! Ventures Pitch Competition in both Stage 1 and Stage 2 in 2019**.

My responsibilities included customer discovery, fundraising, assisting in prototype development, and presenting to potential stakeholders.
