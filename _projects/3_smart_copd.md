*January 2021 – December 2021 · [smartcopd.mkmachado.com](https://smartcopd.mkmachado.com) (ML functionality disabled in demo)*

I designed the Smart COPD Patient Monitoring System, a web app that links chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) patients with healthcare providers and predicts the risk of a patient returning to the hospital.

The system features **Google OAuth 2.0**, a **Vue.js** frontend, a **Java** backend, a **PostgreSQL** database, and a **Python** machine-learning module backed by a decision-tree model. I architected the system, managed developers, facilitated communication between advisers, and developed across the full stack.
