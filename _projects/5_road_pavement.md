*May 2020 – August 2020 · ATLSS Engineering Research Center, Lehigh REU*

I helped develop an Android mobile application that crowdsources road-pavement data using camera and accelerometer signals. The app uses a **MobileNet-SSD** image classifier to identify **eight types of road degradation** at various coordinates with a confidence level of 60%.

I contributed to a technical report that was published, and presented the project at the **CIAMTIS** conference.
