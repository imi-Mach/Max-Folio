*September 2020 · MIT Hackathon · [qolab.mkmachado.com](https://qolab.mkmachado.com)*

My peers and I participated in the 2020 MIT Hackathon to produce **QoLab**: the Lo-fi beats study application that lets individuals create music sessions with their friends to conquer work together.

The application is built on the **Django** framework with a **Vue** frontend.
